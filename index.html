About Me

My name is Scott McDowell. I am a native of Colorado where I have lived for 49 years. I grew up in Lakewood and went to Bear Creek High School and later attended Colorado State University for college. I majored in accounting and finance.

I currently live in Castle Rock, Colorado. I have a wife and four beautiful daughters. I am the CFO for a Real Estate Investment Company called Continental Realty Advisors.

Growing up I watched the Denver Broncos every Sunday and my hero was John Elway
